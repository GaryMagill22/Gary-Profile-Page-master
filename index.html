About Me

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer based in Denver, CO with a unique background in diesel mechanics. After spending 15 years as a diesel technician, I decided to pursue my passion for web design and technology. I've always been fascinated by how things work under the hood, and that curiosity has driven me to become a skilled problem solver. Now, as a software developer, I bring that problem-solving experience to every project I work on. I love the challenge of taking an idea and turning it into a tangible product that people can use and enjoy. Outside of coding you can usually find me on the golf course, in the mountains Snowboarding or hiking with my dogs.

After thorough research, I made a decision to join an immersive coding bootcamp at Coding Dojo. Coding Dojo's comprehensive curriculum covers multiple coding languages and stacks, including front-end, back-end, and full-stack development. Programming languages I learned include Python, JavaScript and C#. In addition to programming languages, I learned alot about frameworks and tools such as Node.js, React, Django and Angular as well as databases such as SQL and NoSQL. At the end of the 16 weeks I will have experience in three full technology stacks such as: the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js), and the Java stack.
