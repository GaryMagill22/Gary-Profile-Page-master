About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer based in Denver, CO with a unique background in diesel mechanics. After spending 15 years as a diesel technician, I decided to pursue my passion for web design and technology. I've always been fascinated by how things work under the hood, and that curiosity has driven me to become a skilled problem solver. Now, as a software developer, I bring that problem-solving experience to every project I work on. I love the challenge of taking an idea and turning it into a tangible product that people can use and enjoy. Outside of coding you can usually find me on the golf course, in the mountains Snowboarding or hiking with my dogs.

After careful consideration, I enrolled in the intensive coding bootcamp at Coding Dojo. Throughout this rigorous program, I became proficient in multiple programming languages, including Python, JavaScript, and Java. My skill set expanded to include various frameworks and tools such as Node.js, React, Flask, Django, JQuery, and JavaSpring. I also deepened my understanding of databases, mastering both MySQL and NoSQL. Over the 16-week period, I became well-versed in three major technology stacks, the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js), Java stack (Java-Spring Boot-MySQL) and Python stack (Django, MySQL, Ajax). This immersive experience has equipped me with a robust set of skills, enabling me to tackle diverse software development challenges.
